Instant Pot Mashed Potatoes

"Fuwa fuwa so tasty!"

A yummy treat, perfect for a thanksgiving dinner!

Ingredients
  • 1.25lbs Yukon Gold Potatoes
  • 1.5 cups of Water
  • 2 cubes of Chicken Bouillon
  • 0.5 cup of Sour Creap
  • 0.5 cup of unsalted butter, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons of milk
    •
    Steps
    1. Cut potatoes, add to pressure cooker and sautee for 4 minutes
    2. Mix potatoes and water and break up bouillon cubes
    3. Close lid, set timer for 10 minutes
    4. Natural release, remove pot and drain most liquid
    5. Add sour cream, butter, and milk. Gently mash!
