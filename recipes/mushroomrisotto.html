Instant Pot Mushroom Risotto

"Omomomo I love mushrooms!"

Say whattt risottos are French?!

Ingredients
  • 0.25 cup unsalted butter
  • 0.25 cup olive oil
  • 3 cups diced mushroom
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 spring rosemary
  • 1.5 cups Arborio rice
  • 0.75 cup white wine
  • 1 quart chicken stock
  • salt and black pepper
  • 0.5 cup grated Parmesan cheese
    •
    Steps
    1. Melt butter and olive oil, sautee mushrooms until softened
    2. Stir rice into pot until grains coated in butter-olive oil
    3. Pour in wine, simmer for 3 minutes
    4. Pour in chicken stock, simmer for 1 minute
    5. Close lid, set timer for 6 minutes
    6. Release pressure, stir risotto until creamy
    7. Season with salt, pepper, rosemary, stir in parmesan cheese
